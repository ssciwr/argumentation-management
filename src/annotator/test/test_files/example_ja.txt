Kagaku sofutou~easentā wa, saigen-sei no aru kagaku to sofutō~ea no jizoku kanōsei o kakuho suru tame ni, kagaku sofutō~ea kaihatsu no kaizen ni tsutomete imasu. SSC wa, samazamana kagaku bun'ya-kan no rinku to shite mo kinō shi, koraborēshon to gakusai-tekina kenkyū o kanō ni shimasu. Kenkyū komyuniti ni okeru sofutō~ea no genzai no yakuwari sofutō~ea kaihatsu wa kenkyū no masumasu jūyōna bubundesuga, jizoku kanō ni okonawa renakereba, kekka wa shibashiba iji fukanōna sofutō~ea to saigen fukanōna kagaku ni narimasu. Kore wa, kagaku-sha-muke no sofutou~eaenjiniaringu no torēningu ga fusoku shite iru koto, kizon no sofutō~ea o iji suru tame no shikin ga kagira rete iru koto, oyobi sofutō~ea kaihatsu-sha no jōnin-shoku ga hotondo nai koto ni yoru monodesu. SSC wa, kaihatsu, kyōiku, autorīchi no 3ttsu no hashira o jissō suru koto ni yori, genzai no ketten ni taisho shimasu.
